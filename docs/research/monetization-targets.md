# Monetization-optimized charter targets (research, 2026-07-08)

Feeds the charter drafter (`proposeCharter`) and the per-format release-plan
presets (BACKLOG #17/#6). The AI previously defaulted conservative ("10k subs /
4000 watch hours / 12 months" = just-get-monetized). These are the
revenue-optimized, research-backed defaults instead.

## Monetization thresholds (2025-26)
- **YPP (standard):** 1,000 subs + **4,000 public watch hours** (12 mo), OR
  1,000 subs + **10M Shorts views** (90 days).
- **Early-access tier:** 500 subs + 3,000 watch hours (or 3M Shorts views) —
  unlocks memberships/Super Chat/shopping, not ad revenue yet.
- AI content **must be disclosed** or it's excluded from ad revenue.

## What drives revenue (ranked)
1. **Niche/advertiser CPM — 10–50x lever.** Finance/AI/B2B ($8–$35 RPM) vs
   entertainment/gaming ($1–$3). Niche beats everything.
2. **Audience geography — up to 47x** (US $32 CPM vs India $0.50). Target Western.
3. **Video length ≥8 min** unlocks mid-roll ads → 2–3x RPM. 10–15 min ideal.
4. **Watch-time / retention** (45%+ retention ≈ 2.5x a 25% channel) and session time.
5. **CTR** (a 1.2→1.6% lift ≈ +33% revenue). Subs are indirect (enable consistency).
6. **Shorts pay ~20–30x less per view** than long-form ($0.01–$0.06 vs $1–$10 per
   1k). Per-watch-hour is ~parity, but per-view is not. Use Shorts to GROW,
   long-form to EARN.

## Recommended default targets (drafter should propose these)

**Long-form (8–15 min, high-CPM niche):**
- 6-mo: 1,000 subs · 4,000 watch hrs · ~$100–400/mo (YPP)
- 12-mo: ~6,000 subs · ~14,000 watch hrs · 3M+ views · $2.5–5k/mo (finance/AI)
- Cadence 4–5/wk · length 10–15 min · retention 45%+

**Hybrid (RECOMMENDED — fastest to YPP + sustainable):**
- 3 long (10–15 min) + 2 Shorts / wk
- 6-mo: ~1,200 subs · 4,000 hrs · YPP · $200–600/mo
- 12-mo: ~10,000 subs · ~20,000 hrs · $1.5–3.5k/mo

**Shorts-only (growth, not revenue — warn the operator):**
- 6-mo: ~2,500 subs · 10M views (YPP via Shorts) · ~$100–200/mo
- 12-mo: ~12,000 subs · 50M+ views · ~$800–1.2k/mo (marginal). Pivot to hybrid.

## Timeline reality
- Consistent faceless: **YPP in ~6 months** (realistic 7–10 with production
  delays); ~$1k/mo another 3–6 months out.
- "30-video rule" — algorithm traction inflects around videos 30–40.
- The "valley" (months 4–6) is where 97% quit — revenue still $0 while growth compounds.

## Beyond ad revenue (60–70% of successful creators' income)
- **Affiliate** — from 0 subs, ~$4.70/1k views; pinned comment 6.3% CTR. Layer immediately.
- **Memberships/Patreon** — from 1k subs.
- **Digital products** — from ~5k subs (a single $497 course ≈ 40–165k views of AdSense).
- **Sponsorships** — from ~10k subs, $1k–10k+/deal (faceless ~20–30% discount).
